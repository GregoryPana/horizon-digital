# Services

## What we do
Horizon Digital builds custom, clear, fast websites for Seychelles businesses. Each site is designed to be easy to navigate, easy to find, and focused on helping visitors take action.

## Who this is for
We work with local businesses that want a professional online presence and a simple path for customers to contact or book. Typical industries include hospitality, retail, services, and professional firms.

## Core services
### Website build
Structured planning, custom design, and a clean launch.

### Design refresh
Modernise your site without losing what already works.

### Mobile-friendly layout
Clear and easy to use on every screen size.

### Speed and stability
Fast load times and a smooth experience for visitors.

### Search-ready setup
Basic on-page setup so people can find you in search.

### Clear customer paths
Guides visitors to contact you without confusion.

## What “custom” means
Your website is built around your goals, content, and audience. Starter and Growth packages are not templates. Foundation uses a structured Horizon Digital layout with limited customisation.

## What we can add
We can add WhatsApp integration, contact forms, booking or enquiry forms, Google Maps, and other scoped add-ons.
