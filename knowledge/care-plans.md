# Care Plans and Support

## Stabilisation (included)
Every project includes a 30-day stabilisation period after launch.

Covers:
- Bug fixes
- Minor corrections
- Small adjustments

Does not cover:
- New features
- Redesigns

## Care plans
### Basic Care
**SCR 1,200 - 2,000 per month**

Includes:
- Security check
- Backup monitoring
- Minor updates
- Email support

Note: Does not include structural changes or search optimisation.

### Growth Care
**SCR 3,000 - 4,500 per month**

Includes:
- Up to 2 hours of structured updates
- Search visibility improvements
- Google presence review
- Performance optimisation
- Simple monthly summary

### Full Care
**SCR 6,000 - 10,000 per month**

Includes:
- Up to 4 hours of improvements
- Ongoing search optimisation
- Content expansion support
- Monthly reporting
- Conversion improvement suggestions
- Priority scheduling

Notes:
- 3-month minimum commitment
- Unused time does not roll over

## Ad-hoc support
**SCR 1,200 per hour**

Available for clients not on a care plan. All work is scoped and approved before starting. Subject to scheduling availability. Care plan clients receive priority scheduling.
